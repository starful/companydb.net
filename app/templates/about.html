About CompanyDB

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the information gap between Japan's high-quality Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) and the global business community. We believe that countless opportunities for partnership, investment, and sourcing are lost simply because valuable information is not easily accessible. CompanyDB was created to solve this problem.

What We Do

CompanyDB is a strategic intelligence platform that leverages cutting-edge AI, including Google's Gemini models, to analyze publicly available data on premium Japanese SMEs. We transform raw data into in-depth, actionable B2B reports in English, focusing on aspects crucial for international business, such as "Monozukuri" (craftsmanship), verified government subsidies, and regional strengths.

Who We Are For

This platform is designed for global investors, sourcing managers, market researchers, and any business professional seeking to discover and understand the hidden champions of the Japanese economy.
